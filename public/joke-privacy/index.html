Privacy Policy — Free Joke Generator

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Free Joke Generator does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

When you tap "Get a joke," the app sends a request to a third-party joke API (icanhazdadjoke.com) to retrieve joke content. No personal or device-identifying information is sent as part of this request beyond what is technically necessary to complete a standard web request.

This app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device permissions.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact ethinruesch@gmail.com.
